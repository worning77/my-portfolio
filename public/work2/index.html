This is the story of my internship — design the Me Page of 6.0 version.

When I finished my unsuccessful last startup experience, I decided to take some internship in Tech company to develop my all-around skills, since the biggest reason for my failure was unprofessional. Luckily, I found one of my alumni’s company was hiring, and their App was right in the social network field. During the intern, I almost involved in every part of the company, the design process and using data spider…

Me Page - LesPark

Background

In 2016, the live stream had gone viral in China. Basically, every social media company found its ability to make money quickly and started adding this function to their Apps after some previous successful cases since the end of 2015. Two months before I entered my company, LesPark—a worldwide lesbian social App had made live as its core function.

Because LesPark had a large number of users in China mainland and Taiwan, camgirl seemed to be suitable for this platform very well. The outdated interfaces and the terrible details which were full of bugs, however, made the App hard to use as well as hard to attract new users and easy to lose current users.

After half a month I joined the company and acknowledged the team, my leader( the only one PM ) and I led the whole team to design the brand new 6.0 version, aiming to uniform the whole style and add the live function without compromising the original social attribute.

My Role and Achievement

  • Since the only PM was focusing on improving the live function and company’s profits, I, as the product assistant, did a lot of work to find and solve the widespread little errors in the App.

  • It was not uncommon to spot that Chinese small company didn’t have UX designer, and all the design work was done by the UI designers, which brought them a lot of workloads. Under my suggestion to my boss, I became a UX designer to help bridge the development and design team, meanwhile, I can still receive needs from the marketing team.

  • During the whole process of design and develop the new version, I found many communicational issues between each team, which caused too much over and over again poorly done work. I told to my boss and worked with him to make a design-process norm, including the main involved people, which indeed improved our later efficiency. (For some reasons, I can't show them clearly)

    Redefine and Restore

    In this case, Me Page should contain three main parts: social, live and settings:

    As for social part: LesPark has been released for a long time, most of its main social functions, like special Album entrance, were outdated, which seemed to be complicated and tedious. I was trying to make it clear and reduce users’ operation.

    As for new live function: In order to catch the live trend before, the previous PM added some direct entrance to increase profits, like redeem and pay bills, but he forgot to make these orderly. I need to create a system to make them reasonable.

    As for settings: For a long time, every PM didn’t notice the arrangement of settings, which seemed messy from today’s perspective. I had to restore those necessary functions in other way.

    Social part--Brief target users research

    One day, I gathered some girls from marketing and host operation teams (they are lesbian) to hold a meeting. For collecting useful information, I listened to them sharing their favorite LGBT App, what parts they cared about most when saw a stranger and what they hoped to show and post to the public.

    I found that the sequence of information they cared was:

  • Photos (more is better)
  • Basic info
  • Popularity
  • Posts
  • Detailed info

    • For those who liked watching live streams, they also cared about their idols' live info -- level and related groups, which were quite familiar with the situations in real life.
    Thus, after they helped me to fliter the most important elements, the only thing I needed to do was to design it as beautiful as possible.

    Reflection

  • 整理是关键：对于个人页面，不同领域的平台有不同的分类方法，他们的存在都是有意义的。在这里为了将直播利益最大化同时还要不失社交的原本属性。我起码在这次挑战中并没有单一的去抄袭他人，而是通过思考完成了我的任务。

  • 高质量的平台不一定能挣钱，而能挣钱的平台一定需要优秀的用户体验去维持：我们的竞品：热拉，一直主导高质量颜值用户，给予LGBR用户归属感，但是他就是做不到盈利（如同知乎，到现在都没有变现能力）。精英的圈子限制了直播这个盈利手段。而我们平台在庞大的用户支持下，很快吸引到愿意看直播的用户，我们设计团队的工作就是将用户体验增强。那次更新之后，新的逻辑界面，先进的直播方式，以及运营团队的主播维持下，我们盈利破百万，顺利拿到新一轮投资。不敢说提升的用户体验的贡献有多大，但是绝对是必要的。

  • 感谢老板给我极大地自由权限，让我能在小公司施展拳脚，在解放主PM的同时，还能有话语权去完善一些细节。虽说我不是专业设计出身，但是我快速学习能力让我完成了设计流程制定。

  • 这次实习让我学习到了在创业中期的一些规则和流程，同时我也发现了自己的所爱：ux design
